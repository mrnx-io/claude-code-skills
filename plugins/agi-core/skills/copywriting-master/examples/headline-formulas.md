# Headline Formulas

Fill-in-the-blank templates for high-converting headlines.

---

## The 12 Core Formulas

### 1. Who Else Wants

**Formula**: "Who Else Wants [Desirable Outcome]?"

**Templates**:
- "Who Else Wants to [ACHIEVE GOAL]?"
- "Who Else Wants [SPECIFIC BENEFIT] Without [COMMON OBSTACLE]?"
- "Who Else Wants to [VERB] Like [ASPIRATIONAL FIGURE]?"

**Examples**:
- "Who Else Wants to Retire 10 Years Early?"
- "Who Else Wants Six-Pack Abs Without Giving Up Beer?"
- "Who Else Wants to Write Like Stephen King?"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #6 (superiority), #5 (comfort), #8 (social approval)

---

### 2. They Laughed When I

**Formula**: "They Laughed When I [ACTION], But When I [RESULT]..."

**Templates**:
- "They Laughed When I [STARTED SOMETHING], But When I [ACHIEVED OUTCOME]..."
- "Everyone Mocked My [DECISION]. Then I [REVEALED RESULT]."
- "[SKEPTICS] Said It Couldn't Be Done. Then [PROOF]."

**Examples**:
- "They Laughed When I Sat Down at the Piano, But When I Started to Play..."
- "They Laughed When I Quit My Job. Then They Saw My Bank Statement."
- "My Boss Said I'd Never Make It. Last Month I Bought His Company."

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #6 (superiority), #8 (social approval)

---

### 3. Do You Make These Mistakes

**Formula**: "Do You Make These [NUMBER] Mistakes in [AREA]?"

**Templates**:
- "Do You Make These [#] Mistakes in [YOUR FIELD]?"
- "Are You Guilty of These [#] [NEGATIVE TRAIT] Habits?"
- "[#] Mistakes [TARGET AUDIENCE] Make Every Day"

**Examples**:
- "Do You Make These 7 Mistakes in Email Marketing?"
- "Are You Guilty of These 5 Money-Burning Habits?"
- "9 Mistakes New Freelancers Make Every Week"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #3 (fear avoidance), #6 (superiority)

---

### 4. How to

**Formula**: "How to [ACHIEVE BENEFIT] [QUALIFIER]"

**Templates**:
- "How to [ACHIEVE RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]"
- "How to [SOLVE PROBLEM] Without [SACRIFICE]"
- "How to [ACTION] and [ACTION] at the Same Time"

**Examples**:
- "How to Lose 20 Pounds in 30 Days"
- "How to Build a 6-Figure Business Without Quitting Your Job"
- "How to Eat Dessert and Still Lose Weight"

**LF8 Fit**: Universal—works with all 8

---

### 5. The Secret of

**Formula**: "The Secret of [DESIRABLE OUTCOME]"

**Templates**:
- "The Secret of [SUCCESSFUL GROUP]"
- "The Hidden Secret Behind [ACHIEVEMENT]"
- "[ASPIRATIONAL GROUP]'s Best-Kept Secret"

**Examples**:
- "The Secret of Six-Figure Freelancers"
- "The Hidden Secret Behind Warren Buffett's Wealth"
- "Silicon Valley's Best-Kept Hiring Secret"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #6 (superiority), curiosity (secondary)

---

### 6. Warning

**Formula**: "Warning: [DANGER] Could [CONSEQUENCE]"

**Templates**:
- "Warning: [THIS MISTAKE] Could [DEVASTATING RESULT]"
- "Warning: [THING YOU'RE DOING] May Be [NEGATIVE OUTCOME]"
- "[AUTHORITY] Warns: [DANGER]"

**Examples**:
- "Warning: This Common Habit Could Be Destroying Your Retirement"
- "Warning: Your Morning Routine May Be Killing Your Productivity"
- "Doctors Warn: This Popular Food Is Aging You Faster"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #3 (fear), #1 (survival)

---

### 7. Give Me

**Formula**: "Give Me [TIME] and I'll Give You [RESULT]"

**Templates**:
- "Give Me [TIMEFRAME] and I'll [TRANSFORMATION]"
- "[TIMEFRAME] to [RESULT]—Guaranteed"
- "In Just [TIME], You'll [OUTCOME]"

**Examples**:
- "Give Me 30 Days and I'll Give You a Six-Pack"
- "7 Days to Double Your Email Open Rates—Guaranteed"
- "In Just 2 Hours, You'll Speak Conversational Spanish"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #5 (comfort/ease), any with time element

---

### 8. Discover

**Formula**: "Discover the [ADJECTIVE] Way to [BENEFIT]"

**Templates**:
- "Discover the [SURPRISING ADJECTIVE] Way to [ACHIEVE GOAL]"
- "Finally Discover [SECRET/METHOD] That [PROMISES RESULT]"
- "Discover Why [GROUP] Are [ACHIEVING RESULT]"

**Examples**:
- "Discover the Lazy Way to Build Wealth"
- "Finally Discover the Method That Melts Fat While You Sleep"
- "Discover Why Top CEOs Are Meditating 20 Minutes a Day"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #5 (comfort), curiosity (secondary)

---

### 9. Number of Ways

**Formula**: "[NUMBER] Ways to [SOLVE PROBLEM]"

**Templates**:
- "[#] Ways to [ACHIEVE BENEFIT]"
- "[#] Proven Methods to [OUTCOME]"
- "[#] Simple Steps to [TRANSFORMATION]"

**Examples**:
- "7 Ways to Double Your Close Rate This Week"
- "12 Proven Methods to Eliminate Back Pain"
- "5 Simple Steps to Financial Freedom"

**LF8 Fit**: Universal—specificity increases credibility

---

### 10. At Last

**Formula**: "At Last! [SOLUTION] That Actually [WORKS]"

**Templates**:
- "At Last! A [PRODUCT/METHOD] That Actually [DELIVERS RESULT]"
- "Finally! [SOLUTION] That [OVERCOMES COMMON FAILURE]"
- "At Last: The [THING] That Actually [WORKS]"

**Examples**:
- "At Last! A Diet That Actually Keeps the Weight Off"
- "Finally! A CRM That Salespeople Actually Use"
- "At Last: Marketing That Actually Generates ROI"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #3 (relief from frustration)

---

### 11. What They Don't Tell You

**Formula**: "What [AUTHORITY] Don't Tell You About [TOPIC]"

**Templates**:
- "What [EXPERTS] Don't Want You to Know About [TOPIC]"
- "The Truth [INDUSTRY] Doesn't Want You to See"
- "What [AUTHORITY] Won't Tell You About [THEIR FIELD]"

**Examples**:
- "What Financial Advisors Don't Tell You About Retirement"
- "The Truth Big Pharma Doesn't Want You to See"
- "What Real Estate Agents Won't Tell You About Buying"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #3 (fear), gnosis need (secret knowledge)

---

### 12. Lies About

**Formula**: "[NUMBER] Lies About [TOPIC] That Keep You [PROBLEM]"

**Templates**:
- "[#] Lies About [TOPIC] That Keep You [STUCK/SUFFERING]"
- "The [#] Biggest Lies About [TOPIC]"
- "Stop Believing These [#] Lies About [TOPIC]"

**Examples**:
- "5 Lies About Passive Income That Keep You Broke"
- "The 7 Biggest Lies About Weight Loss"
- "Stop Believing These 3 Lies About Investing"

**LF8 Fit**: Best for #3 (fear), #6 (superiority through knowledge)

---

## Quick Selection Guide

| If Targeting... | Use Formula |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Fear/danger (#3) | Warning, What They Don't Tell You, Lies About |
| Superiority (#6) | They Laughed, Who Else Wants, The Secret of |
| Comfort/ease (#5) | Give Me, Discover, How to |
| Social proof (#8) | Who Else Wants, They Laughed |
| Universal appeal | How to, Number of Ways |

---

## Testing Protocol

1. Write 5-10 headline variations using different formulas
2. Test LF8 appeals: which desire resonates most?
3. A/B split test top 2 performers
4. Winner becomes control; test against it
5. Document what works for this audience
